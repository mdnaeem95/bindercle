Bigger on the inside.

A binder you can carry. A collection you can share. A home for every card you've ever pulled — themed, captioned, and on a page that feels like yours.

Join the TestFlight

Pages, the way you'd arrange them

4-pocket, 9-pocket, 16-pocket — the layouts you already use, miniature in your pocket. Drag to rearrange like Apple's home screen.

Show, not stock

Bindercle is for the binders you'd hand to a friend, not the spreadsheets you'd send to insurance. Themed sets. Cute captions. Aesthetic over inventory.

The shimmer is the point

Native iOS, dark by default. Holo accents where they belong. Pinterest-style discovery feed, plus follow + comments + save for the collectors you actually want to see.

Yours to keep, yours to leave

Delete your account anytime, in-app. Your binders disappear with you. No spreadsheets, no marketing dossier.

Coming soon to TestFlight

Bindercle is in private beta on iOS. If you're a Pokemon TCG collector who'd like to try it before the public release, send a note to pika@bindercle.app with the email attached to your Apple ID and we'll add you to the tester list.

Android comes later. Other TCGs (Magic, Lorcana, sports cards) likely come later still — we want to get Pokemon right first.
